About Me

(Note for resume purpose...) *2001.EXE here, aka Derek Javelosa in real life. I won't show my face here other than in my resume, and I'll probably remove my real name in here after I get any succesful work application. I just prefer being anonymous in all of my art accounts so I apologize for not following formalities. I want be an animator or an illustrator, one way or another. I want to see how far I can take myself as I improve my skills, hoping to enter some studios who animated the shows I grew up loving. My inspirations for my illustrations are Range Murata and Shinkiro as well as the many old-school video game character arts I've seen.
